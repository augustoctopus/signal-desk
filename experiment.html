## Randomization Rationale

Account-level randomization keeps the buying committee on one hypothesis, which avoids cross-contamination between the entry point and the champion inside the same account.

The current send set has 4 accounts and 3 T1-qualified accounts in the broader portfolio.

## Power Calculation

Using a two-sided 5% alpha and 80% power, detecting a reply-rate lift from 5% to 8% requires about 1,059 accounts per arm (2,118 total).

That is far above the four-account demo run, so this experiment brief is for instrumentation and readiness only, not statistical inference.
